Keefy's Guide!

Howdy there! My name is Doctor Keefy And with all the new members coming to Guilded, I wanted to make a good impression. Thus, I have made you a COMPLETE guide to Guilded, with pros, Counts benefits, and anything you'll need along your journey on Guilded

Goodluck and Godsend, people of Guilded


Guilded benefits are listed below(SHOUT OUT TO EdiValentine101 FOR THESE)

• Guilded doesn't have restrictions or paywalls, animated emojis are free, almost every feature that you see on Discord is here as well plus more as a bonus (they're not all extremely useful but they're nice).

• It has a nice UI, which is a bit small on my PC but great nonetheless (you can also zoom the page by pressing "CTRL" and "+", I personally like how it looks at 110% zoom).

• You can upload files up to 500MB compared to only 8Mb on Discord without NITRO, (even though files upload a bit slower here).

• More channels (Media, Docs, List, Forums, Scheduling, Calendar).

• Server Subscriptions give the server owner 97.5% of the amount of money that was donated.

• Polls & Forums are implemented directly into the application.

• More in-depth profile customization WITHOUT needing to pay for nitro or something like that.

• You can reply to up to 5 different messages by pressing the Reply button on 5 distinct messages
Now it's time for a quick overview of Guilded's Pros/cons(SHOUTOUT TO Dr.Keefy FOR THIS)


Pros!

 ~!~ Guilded has Zero paywalls, meaning you do not NEED nitro

~!~ Guilded has a built-in bot integration maker, which you can use to make custom bots

~!~ Guilded has a customization and easy-to-navigate interface(Use Legacy Nav, if interested), a EPIK profile system and a complete array of channels to chose from!

Cons :(

 ~!~ Guilded is un-optimized, meaning it can be laggy, slow, unresponsive, or buggy at times.

~!~ Guilded is a small platform that lacks most big communities, or lack of many members

~!~ Guilded members can be young, toxic, or rude towards other members.
